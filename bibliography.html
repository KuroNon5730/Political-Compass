Auth-Left & Lib-Left Sources

Robert Conquest

Primary source for facts regarding Soviet industrial quotas and the NKVD.
Leon Trotsky
The Revolution Betrayed
Used for facts regarding the rise of the Soviet Nomenklatura (bureaucracy).
Peter Arshinov
History of the Makhnovist Movement
Foundational text for facts on the Ukrainian Free Territory (Lib-Left).
The Harvard Project
Soviet Social System Archives
Oral histories used for facts on daily life and worker discontent in the USSR.

Auth-Right & Lib-Right Sources

Murray Rothbard
For a New Liberty: The Libertarian Manifesto
Source for the Non-Aggression Principle and market privatization facts.
Friedrich Hayek
The Road to Serfdom
Used for facts regarding spontaneous order and the dangers of central planning.
A. James Gregor
The Ideology of Fascism
Academic source for the mechanics of Corporatism and Auth-Right industrial policy.
Ludwig von Mises
Human Action
Provided facts on Laissez-faire calculation and individual economic choice.
