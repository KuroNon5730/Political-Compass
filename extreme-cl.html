Extreme Center-Libertarian

Radical Individualism: Absolute bodily autonomy, digital sovereignty, and voluntary association.

The Sovereign Self (Facts 01-25)

01

Absolute Bodily Autonomy: No state or collective has any claim over an individual’s physical body for any reason.

02

Cryptographic Sovereignty: The right to use unbreakable end-to-end encryption is considered a fundamental human right.

03

Self-Sovereign Identity: Individuals own their data and digital identities; no central authority can "delete" them.

04

Voluntary Dissociation: The right to instantly exit any community, contract, or social arrangement.

05

Cognitive Liberty: Absolute freedom to alter one’s own consciousness through any means without interference.

Anti-State Decentralization (Facts 26-50)

26

Peer-to-Peer Governance: Replacing laws with smart contracts that individuals opt into voluntarily.

27

Radical Transparency: All institutional data must be public, while all individual data must be private.

28

Open Borders (Individualist): National borders are viewed as illegitimate constraints on human movement.

29

Weaponry Sovereignty: The belief that individuals have a right to any technology required for self-defense.

30

Abolition of Standing Armies: Defense is purely a matter of voluntary, temporary alliances of individuals.
